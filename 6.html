While a trend was not observed between average glucose levels and BMI in both charts, as the max age is increased in the chart for those with stroke, there's a noticeably sharp increase between setting the age range from 55 to 60. This may indicate that once age hits a certain point, the probability of getting a stroke significantly increases.
